Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ArtFlow Studio ("we", "us") handles your information when you use our application InstaPro Publisher ("the App"), a desktop tool that lets you publish and schedule your own content to social platforms such as Pinterest and Instagram.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account authorization data. When you connect a Pinterest or Instagram account, the App receives an OAuth access token that lets it act on your behalf. We do not see or store your social network password.
  • Basic account information. Such as your account ID, username, and the boards/profiles you choose to publish to.
  • Content you choose to publish. Images, captions, links and other material that you select inside the App to post.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you with Pinterest / Instagram via their official APIs.
  • To publish or schedule the content you create, on your instruction.
  • To display your connected accounts and posting history inside the App.

We do not sell your data and do not use it for advertising or profiling.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

InstaPro Publisher is a desktop application. Your access tokens and settings are stored locally on your own computer. We do not maintain a central server that copies your personal content.

4. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with third-party platforms strictly to perform the actions you request. Their handling of your data is governed by their own policies:

  • Pinterest Privacy Policy
  • Instagram / Meta Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention & Revoking Access

Tokens are kept only while your account is connected. You can disconnect an account in the App at any time, or revoke the App's access directly from your Pinterest account settings (Settings → Apps and websites / Authorized apps). Once revoked, the App can no longer access your account.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
